Let’s Talk – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Let’s Talk collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application. The Application is developed and operated by Ashu Hasan.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information to provide chat, communication, and live location sharing features.

a) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • App usage data such as pages visited, time spent, and interaction logs

b) Information You Provide

  • Name or profile details
  • Messages and chat content
  • Contacts you choose to communicate with

2. Location Information (Important Disclosure)

The Application collects foreground and background location data only with your explicit consent to enable live location sharing features.

  • Share live location with selected contacts during active chats
  • Allow continuous location sharing even when the app is closed or not in use

Location data:

  • Is shared only with contacts you choose
  • Is not used for advertising
  • Is not sold to third parties

You can stop location sharing at any time from app settings or by disabling location permissions.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses trusted third-party services that may collect information to improve performance:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Information may be disclosed only:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect user safety and application integrity
  • With trusted service providers working on our behalf

No personal or location data is sold to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide application functionality. Location data is retained temporarily during active sharing sessions.

To request data deletion, contact: ashu.hasan155221@gmail.com

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. If personal data from a child is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are used to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission method is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
